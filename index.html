#home

Hello world, I'm May!

I am a student at the HU University of Applied Sciences Utrecht. The course I'm following is called 'Open-ICT', and unlike the regular IT courses, you work on real-world projects. The primary projects I worked on in the last year were (including the predecessor of) this website, and a game called 'Spark', but I will explain more about that in the projects section.

Now to start telling a bit about myself, I will start with my biggest passion. Which is music, I LOVE music with a passion. On a daily basis, I spend hours listening to all sorts of music, in my opinion, music might actually be the most diverse for of art. Songs can have many different interpretation, it has the power to unite people, and often convey so many emotions in such a short span. Listening to the right type of music can have such a big impact, in the sense of emotions, my productivity, it can really set the scene for a situation.

Now after having shared my love and passion for music, you might be wondering, do you play any instruments or something? To that the answer is… Yes! The instruments I like to play are the electric guitar, and the bass. Aside from that I also enjoy playing around in Logic Pro. Although admittedly, I am far from proficient on any of the instruments I play. But regardless, it’s a lot of, and I could recommend anyone who’s considering picking up an instrument to give it a try!

Another one of my passions is learning new things, I just absolutely love to do research, I can spend countless hours deep diving into “unknown seas of knowledge” and explore them to the depths. Often times I also love to share the knowledge I have attained with my peers, especially if I think it might benefit them as well.

Lastly, I place a lot of value in humor. The primary reason is because (in my unprofessional opinion), it is a amazing way to enlighten otherwise difficult situations. So in a way, it’s also a healthy way to cope with stress!
